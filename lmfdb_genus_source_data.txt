File is colon delimited ":"  Fields are ordered as follows:

0) absolute discriminant (unsigned decimal string) --> curves.disc_key (unsigned decimal string -- first three chars are zero filled number of digits, then disc)
1) conductor (unsigned decimal string) --> curves.cond (int)
2) hash (unsigned decimal string) --> isogeny_classes.hash (int)
3) equation ([f(x),h(x)] --> curves.min_eqn (array of two arrays of integer coeefs)
4) discriminant sign ("1" or "-1") --> curves.disc_sign (int)
5) Igusa-Clebshc invariants (array of 4 signed decimal strings) --> curves.igusa_clebsch (array of 4 signed decimal strings)
6) root number ("1" or "-1") --> isogeny_classes.root_number (int)
7) bad euler factors (comma delimited list of polys in x) --> isogeny_classes.bad_lfactors (array of arrays of integer coeefs)
8) Sato-Tate group (FKRS string label) --> curves.st_group (string)
9) automorphism group (array of unsigned decimal strings -- gap id) --> curves.aut_grp (array of 2 ints)
10) geometric automorphism group (array of unsigned decimal strings -- gap id) -->curves.geom_aut_grp (array of 2 ints)
11) torsion (array of unsigned decimal strings -- cyclic factor orders) --> curves.torsion (array of ints) and curves.torsion_order (int)
12) 2-Selmer rank (unsigned decimal string) --> curves.two_selmer_rank (int)
13) good lfactors (array of arrays of 3 signed decimal strings [p,a1,a2]) --> isogeny_classes.good_lfactors (array of arrays of 3 ints)
